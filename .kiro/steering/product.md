# Product Overview

PaperLens is a research paper intelligence aggregator that provides multi-source academic paper search, deduplication, and ranking in a single tool.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Multi-source search**: Aggregates from 8 academic sources (Crossref, OpenAlex, arXiv, Semantic Scholar, PubMed, DOAJ, Europe PMC, CORE)
- **Intelligent deduplication**: Removes duplicate papers across sources using similarity algorithms
- **Smart ranking**: Combines relevance, citation count, and source reliability
- **Export ready**: JSON, CSV, and BibTeX formats for citation management

## Key Features
- Real-time search across 200M+ papers
- Advanced filtering (year, source, open access, citation count, venue)
- Saved papers management with personal reading lists
- Search alerts and notifications
- Paper detail views with full abstracts and metadata
- Compliance-first approach (API-only, no scraping, polite headers)

## Target Users
- Researchers and academics conducting literature reviews
- Students working on thesis or research projects
- Librarians and information professionals
- Anyone needing comprehensive academic paper discovery
